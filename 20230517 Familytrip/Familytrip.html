Hot Air Balloon Ride

01

Let’s start you journey with enjoying a hot air balloon ride in Taitung to add a touch of luxury to your family vacation in Taiwan. As you soar above the breathtaking landscapes, you'll be treated to stunning panoramic views that will take your breath away. Sipping on champagne or enjoying a light breakfast while floating high above the stunning scenery is an experience that will leave your family feeling pampered and indulged.

Lunch

Coconut Cafe'

02

The kids can play in the crystal-clear waters and build sandcastles on the beach, while you relax and soak up the sun. It's a wonderful spot to enjoy the laid-back vibe of Taiwan's east coast and create unforgettable memories with your family.

Formosan Skia Deer Park

03

A visit to the Luyeh sika deer park is a fantastic family-friendly activity that provides an opportunity to get up close and personal with Taiwan's special sika deer. The park is home to hundreds of these gentle creatures, and visitors can feed them and interact with them in a safe and controlled environment.

Taimali Daylily Mountain

04

Get ready for an unforgettable adventure at Taimali Daylily Mountain! This stunning natural attraction is a must-visit destination for those who want to experience the beauty of Taiwan's outdoors. During the blooming season, the mountain bursts into a kaleidoscope of vibrant daylily flowers that create a breathtaking scene.

Dinner

Sinasera 24

05

Experience a unique dining experience at Sinasera 24, a trendy and stylish restaurant that offers an exquisite fusion of Western and Asian flavors.With excellent service, a unique dining experience, and a prime location in the heart of Taitung, Sinasera 24 is the perfect place to enjoy a memorable dinner with your loved ones.

Hotel

Chihpen Century Hotel

06

Located in the scenic Taitung County, the hotel offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains and the beautiful coastline.The hotel's outdoor swimming pool and hot spring facilities offer the perfect opportunity to rejuvenate and soak in the healing waters after a day of adventure.
