BESTIES

TRIP

one day

Do Yoga in Nature

01

The fresh air and tranquil environment create a calming atmosphere that allows participants to relax and connect with nature. The yoga classes are taught by experienced instructors who guide participants through a mindful practice that cultivates both physical and mental well-being. After the practice, you can also explore the farm and learn about organic farming methods, further immersing themselves in the natural beauty of Taiwan.

Lunch

Le Palais

02

As a Michelin three-star restaurant, Le Palais is known for its exquisite Chinese cuisine, which is presented with artistic flair and precision. The restaurant's elegant and luxurious ambiance, coupled with its attentive service and impeccable attention to detail, creates an unforgettable dining experience.

Cheongsam Dress

03

Cheongsam is a traditional Chinese dress that is form-fitting and adorned with intricate designs, making it perfect for photoshoots.This activity is perfect for those who want to capture their Taiwan cultural experience in a unique and unforgettable way.

Afternoon Tea in Restaurant Bus

04

The bus is a double-decker, providing stunning views of the city as you indulge in delicious cuisine. Along the way, we will take in the sights and sounds of Taipei, making stops at some of the city's most iconic landmarks. With expert guides and top-notch service, the Taipei restaurant bus is a must-try for any foodie visiting the city.

Luxurious Spa

174 Wing Knight Station

05

The spa features a range of treatments and therapies designed to help guests unwind and rejuvenate, from traditional massages and facials to cutting-edge wellness programs. The spa's sleek and modern design, coupled with its state-of-the-art facilities and attentive staff, creates a calming and welcoming atmosphere.

Dinner

CÉ LA VI Taipei

06

CÉ LA VI Taipei offers a unique and luxurious dining and drinking experience for those seeking an upscale night out. Located on the top floor of the iconic Taipei 101building, guests can enjoy stunning views of the city while indulging in gourmet cuisine and creative cocktails.

Hotel

Humble House Taipei

07

Humble House Taipei is a luxurious five-star hotel that combines elegance and sophistication with modern amenities. Its spacious and well-appointed rooms feature floor-to-ceiling windows that offer stunning views of the city.
