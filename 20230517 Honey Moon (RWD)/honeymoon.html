Yangmingshan Park

01

A stunning destination located just outside of the capital city Taipe. This mountain range offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape and is home to a variety of flora and fauna, including cherry blossoms, hot springs, and hiking trails. Our tour will take you on a scenic journey through the mountain and learn about the history and culture of the area.

Lunch

Pine Garden Restaurant

02

A zen-inspired restaurant located in Yagmingshan Mountain that is perfect for relaxation and enjoyment of the food. Pine Garden restaurant serves the best Japanese and Taiwanese cuisine, which has been selected in the Michelin Guide recommended restaurant list.

Hot Spring

03

Step into a blissful retreat in the hot springs of The Gaia Hotel, nestled among the mountains of Beitou. Indulge your body, mind, and spirit in a private hot spring room with stunning views of the natural landscape.

Fly Sky Lantern

04

Pingxi, a charming small town located in the northeast of Taiwan, renowned for its annual Sky Lantern Festival, which draws crowds from all over the world. Formolux travel works with skilled localartisans to design a heart-shaped lantern exclusively for you and your loved one, allowing you to release your dreams into the sky together.

Taipei 101 Night View

05

Taipei 101 is an iconic landmark and one of the tallest buildings in the world. We will take you to the top of Taipei 101 to enjoy the breathtaking beauty of Taipei's skyline at night.

Dinner

Sea to Sky seafood & bar

06

Let’s end the night in the fine dining restaurant “Sea to Sky seafood & bar.”We've collaborated with the restaurant to offer a special romantic dinner for you. Indulge in the freshest seafood with a glass of wine while enjoying the stunning views from the 47th floor.

Shangri-La Far Eastern

Enjoy your romantic night with panoramic views of the city in our five-star hotel “Shangri-La Far Eastern”.
