TRAVEL

one day

Xuan Kong Temple

01

Our first stop will be the Xuan Kong Temple, a beautiful Taoist templelocated in Tainan.The temple is known for its stunning architecture and intricate carvings, which date back to the Qing Dynasty. As part of our special service, we will customize an amulet with the traveler's name by embroidery, which you can bring inside the temple to pray for your good fortune and protection.

Lunch

Lao Guang Cantonese Restaurant

02

It is known for its creative twist on traditional Cantonese cuisine, with a focus on their signature dish, flower wine chicken.The set will feature a variety of the restaurant's best dishes, including their famous flower wine chicken, steamed fish, stir-fried vegetables, and more. You can indulge in the flavors of authentic Cantonese cuisine in a cozy and welcoming setting.

Chimei Museum

03

The museum houses an extensive collection of art and artifacts from around the world, including paintings, sculptures, musical instruments, and rare documents. Visitors can explore the museum's multiple galleries and exhibitions, which offer a glimpse into the rich history and culture of various regions around the world.

Singing Bowl Therapy

04

Singing bowls are a traditional Tibetan instrument used for meditation and relaxation, and their soothing sounds and vibrations are said to promote healing and balance. You will learn how to use the singing bowl and engage in a guided meditation session, surrounded by the tranquil natural beauty of Yuguang Island.The experience will provide a serene and unforgettable moment for our customer, and a chance to connect with the peaceful rhythms of nature.

Dinner

174 Wing Knight Station

05

The menu features a variety of dishes, from traditional Taiwanese cuisine to international favorites, as well as a selection of beverages to complement the meal. The restaurant also boasts a unique transparent sky trail. You can walk along the glass walkway, suspended high above the city, providing an exhilarating and unforgettable experience.

Chikan Herb Bar

06

It is located inside an old Chinese medicine shop, and the bartenders use traditional Chinese herbs and spices to create their signature drinks.You can enjoy the unique ambiance of the bar, sipping on delicious cocktails and immersing yourself in the lively nightlife of Tainan City.

Hotel

Formosa Yacht Resort

07

This stunning resort offers a unique experience for you to relax and unwind in a luxurious setting.Enjoy a comfortable night's rest and wake up to the sounds of the waves crashing on the shore.
